FSSAI LICENSE/REGISTRATION Or FOOD LICENSE

FSSAI is required for all entities who have been involved in the food business whether food manufacture, packaging, distributing or selling. The Registration and Licence serves as proof and authorisation for any food related business to operate as a legal food business entity. Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI), an institution that monitors food businesses under the guidelines and regulations listed in the FSSAI Act 2000.

  • Turnover upto 12 lakhs - only registration is required.
  • Turnover above 12 lakhs to 20 crore - State Licence
  • Turnover above 20 crore - Central Licence.

GST REGISTRATION (GOODS AND SERVICES TAX)

Goods and Services Tax is an indirect tax which is levied at all stages from manufacturing to consumption. It applies to all service providers, traders, manufacturers and consumers. Every business shall have to register under GST if its turnover exceeds from 20 lakhs in a financial year. 40 lakhs in special category and compulsory for E-Commerce entity to obtain GST irrespective of their turnover.
